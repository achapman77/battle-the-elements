Elemental Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four elements below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Total Score.

You win a game by matching your total score to the random Target Number. You lose a game if your total score goes above the Target Number

The numerical value of each element is hidden.

Each time a new game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
